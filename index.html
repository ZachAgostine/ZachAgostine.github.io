Laser Cowboy Shooting Gallery (work in progress)

This project was developed with the help of the electronics engineering club at Wake Tech. An Arduino powered Nerf gun is used to shoot a laser at photo-resistors to test accuracy. My contributions include 3D printing parts, updating blaster code, and connecting electrical components for the targets.
